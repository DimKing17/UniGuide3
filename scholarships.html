OSAP — Ontario Student Assistance Program

OSAP provides a combination of non-repayable grants and repayable loans to Ontario students. Apply early — decisions are released in summer.

  • Grants are based on family income — students from lower-income families can receive up to $10,000/yr non-repayable.
  • Apply at ontario.ca/osap — opens in late spring/early summer before your first year.
  • You need your parents' income tax information and your own SIN number.
  • Even if you think you won't qualify, apply — many students are surprised.
💡 Tip: Students from families earning under $175,000 may be eligible for some grant portion. The earlier you apply, the faster your funding is confirmed.
